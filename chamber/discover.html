Population

There are 75,987 residents in Scranton, with a median age of 37.5. Of this, 50.32% are males and 49.68% are females. US-born citizens make up 86.37% of the resident pool in Scranton, while non-US-born citizens account for 7.52%. Additionally, 6.11% of the population is represented by non-citizens. A total of 62,118 people in Scranton currently live in the same house as they did last year.

Employment

White-collar workers make up 73.99% of the working population in Scranton, while blue-collar employees account for 26.01%. There are also 2,105 entrepreneurs in Scranton (6.3% of the workforce); 22,975 workers employed in private companies (68.8%); and 3,702 people working in governmental institutions (11.09%).

Household

There are a total of 29,892 households in Scranton, each made up of around 2 members. Family establishments represent 55.78% of these Scranton households, while non-family units account for the remaining 44.22%. Additionally, 27.38% of households have children and 72.62% of households are without children.

Income

The average annual household income in Scranton is $61,395, while the median household income sits at $44,161 per year. Residents aged 25 to 44 earn $51,460, while those between 45 and 64 years old have a median wage of $57,959. In contrast, people younger than 25 and those older than 65 earn less, at $23,263 and $38,106, respectively.

Homeowners & Renters

There are 35,034 housing units in Scranton, and the median year in which these properties were built is 0. Of the 29,892 occupied housing units in Scranton, 49.93% are owner-occupied, while 50.07% have renters living in them. Meanwhile, properties bought with mortgages account for 52.53% of the units, and the median value of a home with a mortgage is $118,600. In general, housing costs reach $864 per month in Scranton.

Education

Approximately 45.42% of the population in Scranton holds a high school degree (that's 27,258 residents), while 22.34% have attained a college certificate (12,444 locals) and 13.41% have a bachelor's degree (8,049 people).

Martial Status

A total of 27,001 people in Scranton have never been married (which represents 42.9% of the total population), while 24,311 of them are wedded (38.63%). Separated and divorced residents are in smaller numbers, at 1,183 (1.88%) and 7,138 (11.34%), respectively.

Transportation

The top three means of transportation people in Scranton use to get to work are: car, walking and bus or trolley bus. A total of 27,979 residents commute by car, 1,814 prefer going to work by walking and 580 by bus or trolley bus.
