Business/Organization Information
Choose Membership

Non-Profit Membership: No fees

  • Access to business workshops and seminars at discounted rates or for free
  • Invitation to networking events
  • Listing in the chamber's non-profit directory
  • One free advertisement on the chamber website or newsletter

Bronze Membership: Cost between $250 to $1,000 per year

  • All non-profit membership benefits
  • Increased visibility on the chamber website
  • Opportunity to sponsor a chamber event or program
  • Listing in the chamber's bronze member directory
  • Access to member-only events and forums

Silver Membership: Cost between $500 to $2,500 per year

  • All bronze membership benefits
  • Additional advertisement opportunities in the chamber newsletter or on social media platforms
  • Invitation to attend VIP events with other business leaders in the community
  • Listing in the chamber's silver member directory
  • Access to exclusive programs such as leadership development and mentorship opportunities

Gold Membership: Cost between $1,000 to $5,000 per year

  • All silver membership benefits
  • Exclusive access to a Chamber Business Concierge service to assist with business needs
  • One-on-one consultation with Chamber staff to discuss business strategies and issues
  • Listing in the chamber's gold member directory
  • Recognition on the chamber website and in the chamber's annual report
  • Invitation to attend and speak at chamber events
