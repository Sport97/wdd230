Target Audience

Our Chamber of Commerce focuses on individuals who seek connections and involvement in the community. Individuals who join us can have opportunities to grow both personally and professionally.

Mother of Three Children

- "As a new resident I struggled to find ways to get involved in our community, that is until I joined the Chamber of Commerce. The connections I've made over the years have helped set an example for my children in ways to help our community strive forward."

High School Student

- "The afterschool events I've been involved in has helped me learn more about our community and counts as credit on my transcript. I can get active experience as I learn."
